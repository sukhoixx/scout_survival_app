Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Scout Survival ("the App") is a reference app for scouts and outdoor enthusiasts providing survival techniques, first aid guidance, and packing checklists. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The App operates entirely offline using content bundled within the app itself. No account, login, or internet connection is required.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No names, emails, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting
  • No camera, microphone, or photo library access
  • No health or fitness data

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
